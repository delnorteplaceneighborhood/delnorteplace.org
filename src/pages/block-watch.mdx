# Block Watch

The Del Norte Place Neighborhood Association has a block watch to foster a sense of community and safety.

The Del Norte Place Block Watch brings neighbors, on each street and each block (16-17th Ave, 15-16th Ave), together to communicate, look out for eachother, and help prevent crime.

> For more information from the City of Phoenix, please see the [City of Phoenix's Block Watch Inforomation](https://www.phoenix.gov/police/resources-information/block-watch-info).
